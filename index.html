about me


My name is Jessica Phyllis Lange, is was born April 20, 1949 (currently 72 years old).

I am an American film actress, winner of two Oscars, four Golden Globe Awards, a Screen Actors Guild Award and three Emmy Awards.

I have 3 children. My oldest daughter Alexandra is 41 years old, Hannah Jane is 37 years old and Walker Samuel is 35 years old. I currently live in New York .
